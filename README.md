# Start Saying More

**Bridging the gap between you and a therapist.**

---

Start Saying More (SSM) is a mental health organization founded in 2020 with one mission: eliminate the barriers that keep Black and minority communities out of therapy — and help them stay once they're in.

56% of African Americans with Major Depressive Disorder don't seek treatment. 51% drop out of therapy within 4 visits. The problem isn't awareness. It's access, trust, and fit. That's what we're here to fix.

---

## What We Do

SSM personally connects people to culturally competent therapists through a hands-on, 1:1 matching process. We take the search off your plate so you can focus on the work.

- Manual therapist matching — founder-led, no algorithms yet
- Free for people seeking therapy
- Focused on Black and minority communities

---

## What We're Building

The SSM website is currently being rebuilt. The goal: a free, personality-driven therapist matching platform where users complete an intake survey and get matched to providers based on who they are, what they've been through, and what they need.

> Currently: manual matching model  
> Next: technology-driven platform
