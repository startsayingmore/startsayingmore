## Voice Rules

These apply to all written and spoken SSM content:

- **No em dashes (—) anywhere.** They read as AI-generated. Use ellipses, commas, or hyphens instead.
- **"lol" is a pressure valve,** not a dismissal. It appears after something heavy to signal self-awareness. Never remove it.
- **Caps for emphasis** — she writes the way she speaks. (LOVE, BEAT, DOWN)
- **Short punchy closes.** Long setup, devastating short landing.
- **No preachy or performative language.** Name the hard thing and own it.
- **Captions:** Hook line first, 2-4 short follow-up lines, end with a question. 5 hashtags max.

### What the voice is NOT:
- Not polished for polish's sake
- Not self-pitying
- Not vague
- Not afraid of a well-placed expletive
