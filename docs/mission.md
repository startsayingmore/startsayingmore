# SSM Mission and Problem Statement

## Mission

Eliminate the search to enlist — and retain — Black and minority people in mental health treatment.

---

## The Problem

Minority groups are not starting or staying in therapy. The barriers are real: stigma, cost, cultural fit, and the sheer friction of finding someone right. SSM exists to remove that friction.

### Major Depressive Disorder (MDD) Prevalence

| Group | MDD Prevalence | % Who Don't Seek Treatment |
|---|---|---|
| African Americans | 10.4% | 56% |
| Caucasians | 17.9% | 38.6% |

### Annual Treatment Rates — U.S. Adults with Any Mental Illness

| Group | Treatment Rate |
|---|---|
| Non-Hispanic White | 49% |
| Non-Hispanic Black | 31% |
| Non-Hispanic Mixed | 32% |
| Hispanic/Latino | 33% |
| Non-Hispanic Asian | 25% |

### Therapy Dropout Rates (within 4 visits)

| Group | Dropout Rate |
|---|---|
| African American | 51% |
| Asian | 49% |
| White | 40% |
| Latino | 31% |

*Sources: Neuropsychiatric Disease and Treatment; SAMHSA; Psychotherapy (Chic)*

---

## The Solution

A free therapist matching platform that connects users to providers based on:

1. Personality type (Myers-Briggs)
2. Lived experiences and preferences
3. Areas of concern

The matching is personalized, culturally competent, and focused on Black and minority users from day one — not as an afterthought.

### Competitive Advantage

The only platform that combines all of: personality matching, experiences matching, area of concern, personalized match list, focus on Black and minority communities, free access, and 24/7 availability.
